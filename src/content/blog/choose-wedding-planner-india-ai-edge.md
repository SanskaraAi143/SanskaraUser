Hiring a wedding planner for your Indian wedding isn't a luxury; it's an investment in your peace of mind. They are the architects of your dream, the conductors of your chaotic symphony, and the calm in your celebratory storm. But in a country filled with incredible creative talent, finding the *right* planner—someone who gets your vision, respects your traditions, and vibes with your personality—is the most important first step.

This guide will not only walk you through the essential steps of hiring a wedding planner in India but also reveal a modern secret weapon: combining their human expertise with the precision of an AI tool like Sanskara AI. This powerful duo creates a planning experience that is both deeply personal and flawlessly efficient.

## Step 1: The Soul-Searching - What Do You *Really* Want?

Before you even type "wedding planner" into Google, have an honest conversation with your partner.

*   **What's the Vibe?** Are you dreaming of a grand, royal affair, an intimate bohemian gathering, or a chic, modern celebration?
*   **What's Your Involvement Level?** Do you want to be involved in every tiny detail, or would you rather hand over the reins and just show up for the fun parts? This will determine if you need full planning, partial planning, or day-of coordination.
*   **What's the Budget?** Be realistic about what you can spend, not just on the wedding itself, but on the planning services. Planners typically charge a flat fee, a percentage (10-15%) of the wedding budget, or a combination.

## Step 2: The Hunt - Finding Your Shortlist

Now, it's time to find your potential partners in planning.

*   **Instagram is Your Lookbook:** Search hashtags like `#IndianWeddingPlanner`, `#DelhiWeddings`, or `#DestinationWeddingIndia`. Look for planners whose aesthetic makes your heart skip a beat.
*   **Word-of-Mouth is Gold:** Talk to friends, family, and colleagues who have recently gotten married. Their firsthand experiences are invaluable.
*   **Wedding Blogs & Magazines:** Reputable publications often feature the work of top-tier planners.

Create a shortlist of 3-5 planners who seem like a good fit.

## Step 3: The Interview - Asking the Right Questions

This is more than an interview; it's a chemistry check. Come prepared with smart questions that go beyond the surface.

### The "Do You Get Us?" Questions:
*   "Looking at our mood board/Pinterest, how would you describe our style?"
*   "We are a [Your Culture/Region, e.g., Tamil-Punjabi] couple. What's your experience in blending these specific traditions?"
*   "Our top priority is [e.g., guest experience, amazing food, a killer party]. How would you ensure that's the focus?"

### The "How Do You Work?" Questions:
*   "Walk us through your planning process from start to finish."
*   "How do you handle budget tracking and payments? What tool do you use?"
*   **"We're using Sanskara AI to manage our guest list and mood boards. Are you open to collaborating on a digital platform to keep everything centralized?"** (A forward-thinking planner will love this!)

### The "What If?" Questions:
*   "Tell us about a time a wedding didn't go as planned. What happened, and how did you fix it?"
*   "What's your contingency plan for bad weather for an outdoor event?"
