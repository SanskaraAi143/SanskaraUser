## What Does an "AI-Friendly" Venue Really Mean?

In the context of a Bangalore wedding, an "AI-friendly" venue is one that embraces technology to create a smoother, more efficient experience for everyone. It's not just about having Wi-Fi; it's about a mindset. These venues are:

*   **Digitally Collaborative:** They are comfortable receiving and working with digital floor plans, guest lists, and timelines generated by platforms like Sanskara AI.
*   **Tech-Equipped:** They have the infrastructure to support modern needs, from seamless live-streaming for your international guests to reliable connections for your coordination team.
*   **Flexible and Adaptable:** They understand that a Hindu wedding isn't a single event but a series of ceremonies, and their spaces can be transformed to suit each one, from an intimate Haldi to a grand Sangeet.
